Global Food Inefficiency is a Financial and Environmental Crisis

🌍 $1.1T Annual Global Economic Loss from Food Waste
⚖️ 1.05B Tonnes of Food Wasted Annually Worldwide
🏠 25-30% Household Grocery Budget Lost Monthly to Spoilage
💸

The Economic Leak

📊
Global Waste: 1.05 billion tonnes of food wasted annually — equivalent to $1.1 Trillion economic loss
🏠
Household Impact: Average urban household "throws away" 25%-30% of grocery budget every month due to spoilage and poor planning
📈

Inflation Crisis & Sustainability Gap

🌡️
The Inflation Crisis (Regional): In markets like Bangladesh, food inflation is volatile at 9%-12%+. Households spending more for less value with zero tools to track local bazaar price fluctuations in real-time
🌱
The Sustainability Gap: Domestic food waste responsible for 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions. Reducing waste isn't just a "green" goal — it's a massive financial recovery opportunity
